# Privacy Policy — Classic Remote

**Last updated:** March 2026

## What data we collect

Classic Remote does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All TV control commands are sent directly from your device to your Philips TV on your local network. No data leaves your home network.

## Advertising (free version)

The free version displays banner ads provided by **Google AdMob**.

AdMob may collect:
- **Advertising identifier (IDFA)** — only with your permission (App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS 14.5+)
- **IP address** — for approximate location-based ad targeting
- **Device information** — model, OS version, screen size

You can deny tracking permission — ads will still appear but will be non-personalized.

See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchase

The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed by **Apple App Store**. We do not have access to your payment information.

Purchase status is verified locally on your device using **RevenueCat**, which stores only anonymous purchase receipts. RevenueCat does not collect personal data.

See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Local network access

The app requires access to your local network to discover and control Philips TVs. This permission is requested at first launch. No network data is sent outside your local network.

## Data retention

No user data is stored on our servers. TV configuration (IP address, port) is stored locally on your device only.

## Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
