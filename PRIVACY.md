# Privacy Policy — Philips Remote

**Last updated:** March 2026

## What data we collect

Philips Remote does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All TV control commands are sent directly from your device to your Philips TV on your local network.

## Advertising (free version)

The free version of the app displays banner ads provided by **Google AdMob**.

AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads. On iOS 14.5+, the app will request permission before tracking. You can deny this permission — ads will still be shown but may be less relevant.

See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchase

The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed by **Apple App Store**. We do not have access to your payment information.

Purchase status is verified locally on your device using **RevenueCat**, which stores anonymous purchase receipts.

See RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Local network access

The app requires access to your local network to discover and control Philips TVs. No data is sent outside your network.
